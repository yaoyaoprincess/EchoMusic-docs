# 🎧 Personal FM

Personal FM is EchoMusic's smart radio feature that automatically recommends music based on your listening preferences, helping you discover more great songs.

## What is Personal FM

Personal FM is like a personal music radio station:

- No need to manually select songs — the system plays continuously
- Smart recommendations based on your listening history and preferences
- Gets smarter the more you listen — recommendations become increasingly accurate
- Perfect for when you just want to relax and let the music flow

## How to Use

1. Click **"Personal FM"** on the home page or sidebar
2. The FM player automatically starts playing recommended songs
3. Enjoy a continuous stream of music

## Controls

In FM mode, you can provide feedback on the currently playing song:

| Action | Icon | Description |
|------|------|------|
| Like | ❤️ | Mark as liked — the system will recommend more similar songs |
| Dislike | 🚫 | Skip this song — the system will reduce this type of recommendation |
| Skip | ⏭ | Skip to the next recommended song |
| Pause | ⏸ | Pause FM playback |

Every piece of feedback helps FM better understand your music taste.

::: tip
Click "Dislike" on songs you don't enjoy — FM will immediately skip and reduce similar recommendations. This is the fastest way to train FM's taste to match yours.
:::

## FM vs Normal Playback

| Feature | Personal FM | Normal Playback |
|------|---------|---------|
| Song selection | AI auto-recommendation | Manual selection |
| Playlist | Infinite streaming recommendations | Fixed play queue |
| Feedback | Like / Dislike | None |
| Best for | Casual listening, discovering new music | Listening to specific songs |
| Personalization | Continuous learning & optimization | None |

## Tips

- **When starting with FM**: Use "Like" and "Dislike" often to train the recommendation algorithm
- **After extended use**: Recommendations become more accurate — you'll encounter more songs you enjoy
- **Switching modes**: If you hear a song you love in FM, click the song name to go to its detail page or add it to your favorites
