# 🎤 Music Recognition

EchoMusic features built-in music recognition with both microphone input and system audio capture modes, helping you quickly identify songs playing around you.

## Recognition Modes

### Microphone Recognition

Ideal for identifying music in your environment:

1. Click **"Music Recognition"** on the navigation bar
2. Ensure microphone permission is granted
3. Hold your device close to the audio source
4. Click the **"Start Recognition"** button
5. Wait a few seconds for the result

Use cases:
- Cafes, malls, restaurants, and other public spaces
- Music playing at a friend's gathering
- Background music in TV shows and movies

### System Audio Capture

Ideal for identifying audio playing on your computer:

1. Click **"Music Recognition"** on the navigation bar
2. Switch to the **"System Audio"** tab
3. Click **"Start Recognition"**
4. The system captures the currently playing audio for identification

Use cases:
- Identifying songs playing in web pages
- Identifying background music in videos
- Identifying songs heard in live streams

## Recognition Results

On successful recognition, you'll see:

- **Song Name** — The matched song title
- **Artist** — Performer information
- **Cover** — Album cover art
- **Action Options**:
  - 🎵 Play Now
  - ➕ Add to Play Queue
  - ❤️ Add to Favorites
  - 📋 Copy song info

## Recognition History

All recognition records are saved locally for easy review:

- View history on the "Music Recognition" page
- Support for clearing individual or all history records
- History is stored locally only and is never uploaded to the cloud

## Tips

| Tip | Description |
|------|------|
| Reduce ambient noise | In microphone mode, get as close to the audio source as possible |
| Ensure stable network | Recognition requires an internet connection |
| Choose the right segment | Aim for the chorus section for higher recognition accuracy |
| Moderate volume | Too loud or too quiet may affect accuracy |

::: warning
Microphone recognition requires microphone permission. On macOS, the system will prompt for permission on first use — select "Allow".
:::

## Privacy

- Audio captured during recognition is used solely for fingerprint matching
- Audio clips are not saved after recognition completes
- All data transmission is through encrypted channels
