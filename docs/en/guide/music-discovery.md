# 🔍 Music Discovery

EchoMusic offers rich music discovery features to help you find your next favorite song.

## Home Recommendations

When you open EchoMusic, the home page shows personalized music recommendations:

- **Recommended Playlists**: Smart recommendations based on your listening preferences
- **New Songs**: Latest trending songs
- **Popular Artists**: Currently trending artist recommendations

## Charts

Browse various music charts to see what's trending:

On the "Discover" page, you can view:

| Chart | Description |
|------|------|
| Rising Chart | Songs with surging play counts recently |
| New Song Chart | Latest trending new releases |
| Hot Song Chart | Most popular songs overall |
| Original Chart | Top original works |

Click any chart to enter its detail page and browse the full song list.

## Artists

Discover music by artist:

1. Go to **Discover** → **Artists**
2. Filter by:
   - **Region**: Chinese, Western, Japanese/Korean, etc.
   - **Genre**: Pop, Rock, Folk, Electronic, etc.
3. Click any artist to view:
   - Popular songs
   - Album list
   - Similar artist recommendations

## Albums

Explore complete music albums:

1. Go to an artist's detail page or search
2. Browse the album list
3. Click an album to view:
   - Album cover & description
   - Complete track list
   - Release information

## Playlists

A vast collection of playlists for every occasion:

- **Official Playlists**: Editor-curated playlist recommendations
- **Category Browsing**: Browse by genre, scene, mood
- **Playlist Details**: View complete song list, play all with one click

### Playlist Actions

| Action | Method |
|------|------|
| Play All | Click the **Play All** button on the playlist page |
| Save Playlist | Click ❤️ to save to "My Playlists" |
| Share Playlist | Copy the link to share with friends |

## Music Recommendations

EchoMusic continuously optimizes recommendations based on your listening history:

- The more you listen, the more accurate the recommendations
- Recommendations appear in "Recommended Playlists" on the home page
- Personal FM provides a more immersive recommendation experience — see [Personal FM](/en/guide/personal-fm)
