# 🎵 Playback

EchoMusic provides professional-grade playback controls, letting you enjoy music in the most comfortable way.

## Basic Controls

### Play / Pause

- Click the **Play / Pause** button on the bottom player bar
- Press the **Space** key (when the app has focus)
- Use system media keys (macOS Touch Bar, keyboard media keys)

### Switching Tracks

- **Previous**: Click the ⏮ button on the player bar
- **Next**: Click the ⏭ button on the player bar
- Use system media shortcut keys

### Volume

- Drag the **volume slider** on the player bar to adjust volume
- Click the volume icon to quickly **mute / unmute**
- Use system volume keys for synced adjustment

### Progress Control

- Click or drag the **progress bar** on the player bar to jump to any position
- Current playback time and total duration are displayed in real time

## Play Queue

The play queue manages your current and upcoming song list.

### Queue Operations

| Action | Method |
|------|------|
| Add to queue | Right-click a song in any list → **Add to Play Queue** |
| Remove song | Right-click in the queue → **Remove** |
| Clear queue | Click the **Clear** button at the bottom of the queue |
| Reorder | Drag songs in the queue to rearrange |

::: tip
The play queue is saved locally and will be restored when you restart the app.
:::

## Play Modes

EchoMusic supports multiple play modes. Click the mode button on the player bar to switch:

| Mode | Icon | Description |
|------|------|------|
| Sequential | ▶️ | Plays the queue in order, stops after finishing |
| List Loop | 🔁 | Plays the queue in order, loops from the beginning after finishing |
| Single Loop | 🔂 | Repeats the current song |
| Shuffle | 🔀 | Shuffles the queue randomly |

## Speed Control

Supports playback speed from 0.5x to 2.0x:

1. Click the **speed** button on the player bar
2. Select your desired playback speed
3. 1.0x is normal speed

Use cases:
- **0.5x - 0.75x**: Learning song details, singing practice
- **1.25x - 1.5x**: Quick browsing of songs
- **2.0x**: Fast skimming

## Crossfade

EchoMusic supports smooth crossfade transitions:

- When switching tracks, the current song fades out while the next fades in
- Creates a natural transition, reducing jarring cuts
- Adjust crossfade duration in [Audio Settings](/en/guide/audio)

## Mini Mode

Mini Mode shrinks the player into a compact mini window, ideal for saving screen space while listening in the background:

- Click the **Mini Mode** button on the title bar to switch
- Mini window displays only the cover, song/artist name, and basic playback controls
- The window stays always on top and can be dragged to reposition
- Click the mini window to restore the full interface

Use cases:
- Save screen space while working
- Minimize visual distractions when paired with desktop lyrics
- Keep music control within reach during multitasking
